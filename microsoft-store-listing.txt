----------------------------------------------------------------
FULL DESCRIPTION
----------------------------------------------------------------

Transform the way you consume written content. Texts to
Audiobooks Creator converts your text, documents, and stories
into high-quality audiobooks with natural-sounding narration —
right in your browser, with no software to install.

Whether you're a student juggling lecture notes, a professional
plowing through industry reports, a writer proofreading your
own work, or simply someone who prefers listening over reading,
Texts to Audiobooks Creator puts a personal narrator at your
fingertips.


── PASTE TEXT OR UPLOAD A FILE ──────────────────────────────

Drop in anything you want to hear:
  • Paste text directly into the editor
  • Upload a .TXT file for plain-text documents
  • Upload a .PDF for academic papers, eBooks, or reports
  • Upload a .DOCX for Word documents and manuscripts

The app extracts and prepares your content automatically —
no copy-paste gymnastics required.


── AI-POWERED TEXT CLEANING ─────────────────────────────────

Before narration begins, optional AI text cleaning (powered by
Groq's Llama 3.3 model) tidies up your content for natural
speech: removing page numbers, cleaning up headers, fixing
punctuation, and smoothing transitions. The result is narration
that flows like a real audiobook — not a machine reading a
messy document.

Choose from three narration styles:
  • Storytelling — warm, narrative flow for fiction & essays
  • News — crisp, professional delivery for articles & reports
  • Calm — slow, smooth pacing for meditation & wellness content


── 10 DISTINCT VOICE ACCENTS ────────────────────────────────

Pick the voice that fits your content from 10 curated English
accents across four regions:

  US Voices    — Emma, Guy, Aria, Davis, Jenny
  UK Voices    — Sonia, Ryan
  Australian   — Natasha, William
  Canadian     — Clara

Preview any voice with a live sample before you commit.


── SMART CHAPTER DETECTION ──────────────────────────────────

Long documents are automatically split into chapters. The
built-in chapter navigator lets you jump to any section of your
audiobook instantly — just like a real audiobook player.


── PLAYBACK SPEED CONTROL ───────────────────────────────────

Dial in the perfect listening pace. Adjust narration speed from
0.75× (slow and deliberate) up to 1.5× (rapid review mode).
A live duration estimate updates as you type so you always know
how long your audiobook will be before you generate it.


── YOUR PERSONAL AUDIOBOOK LIBRARY ──────────────────────────

Every audiobook you create is saved to your personal dashboard.
Browse, search, and filter your entire collection by title,
status, or voice. Pick up right where you left off, re-download
any file, or delete projects you no longer need — all from one
clean, organized view.


── DOWNLOAD & KEEP FOREVER ──────────────────────────────────

Every finished audiobook is exported as a standard 64 kbps MP3
file. Download it to your device and listen anywhere — in your
car, on your commute, at the gym — completely offline. Batch
export your entire library as a ZIP file with one click.


── INSTALLABLE PROGRESSIVE WEB APP (PWA) ────────────────────

Texts to Audiobooks Creator is a full Progressive Web App. Pin
it to your Start menu or taskbar directly from Microsoft Edge
for a native app experience — complete with its own window,
icon, and offline support. No app store download required;
your browser handles the installation.


── WHO IS THIS FOR? ─────────────────────────────────────────

  Students           — Convert lecture notes and textbooks into
                       audio you can study on the go.

  Professionals      — Listen to reports, white papers, and
                       emails during your commute.

  Authors & Writers  — Proofread your own work by ear and catch
                       errors your eyes miss.

  Accessibility      — Make written content accessible to those
                       with dyslexia, visual impairments, or
                       reading difficulties.

  Language Learners  — Hear correct pronunciation and cadence
                       for texts in English.

  Busy Parents       — Turn bedtime stories and children's books
                       into audio with a tap.


── PRIVACY FIRST ────────────────────────────────────────────

Your documents are processed to generate audio and are never
stored permanently on our servers. Uploaded files are deleted
immediately after text extraction. You own your content.


── SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS ──────────────────────────────────────

  • Windows 10 or later
  • Microsoft Edge, Chrome, or any modern browser
  • Internet connection required for audiobook generation
  • No additional software, plugins, or accounts needed
